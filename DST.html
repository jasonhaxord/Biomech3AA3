    Instructions:

    The "Dowling Smart Tutor" is a study tutor that accompanies you as each problem is attempted. You should be working the solution out on paper while using the program to simulate a real problem solving atmosphere.

    Click on the section you wish to practice from the menu above. Once you have found a question that you wish to attempt, you can enter an answer in the input box on the right and then click on the "check answer" button to see if your answer is correct. If your is correct, you will be prompted to choose the correct units and allowed to see the full solution.

    If you answer a question incorrectly, you will be presented with a list of "Leading" questions. Each leading question has been designed to help you organize your solution in a step-by-step manner. You are encouraged to follow the leading questions in order without skipping over questions. Each leading question has a multiple choice answer so that you can quickly progress to the source of your error. If you answer incorrectly to a leading question, you are presented with the correct answer and an explanation. With the added information from the failed leading question, you should re-attempt the question on your own without choosing any further leading questions. If your answer is still incorrect, you will be advanced to the next leading question and so on.

    The Dowling Smart Tutor only uses one method of solving each question and there are usually several methods that can be used to successfully answer each question. The student is encouraged to use the method they are most familiar with or prefer.
